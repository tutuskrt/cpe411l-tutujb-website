About Our System

Learn how we protect our community

Our Mission

The Community Flood Alert System was developed by CPE 411L students to provide early warning and real-time monitoring of flood conditions in our local area. Our goal is to keep residents safe and informed during severe weather events.

How It Works

Our system uses sensors installed at strategic locations to measure water levels continuously. When water levels reach critical thresholds, automatic alerts are sent to community members, giving them time to prepare and evacuate if necessary.

Key Features

  • Real-time water level monitoring
  • Automatic alert notifications
  • Historical data tracking
  • Community-focused design

Flood Safety Tips

Essential information to keep you safe

Before a Flood

  • Know your evacuation routes
  • Prepare an emergency kit
  • Move valuables to higher ground
  • Stay informed about weather conditions

During a Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Never walk or drive through floodwater
  • Stay away from power lines
  • Follow evacuation orders

After a Flood

  • Wait for authorities to declare it safe
  • Avoid floodwater (may be contaminated)
  • Document damage for insurance
  • Clean and disinfect everything
