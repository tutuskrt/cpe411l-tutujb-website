Flood Safety Tips

Essential information to keep you safe

Before a Flood

  • Know your evacuation routes and emergency contacts
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essentials
  • Move valuables and important documents to higher ground
  • Stay informed about weather conditions and alerts
  • Fill bathtubs and containers with clean water

During a Flood

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Never walk or drive through floodwater
  • Stay away from power lines and electrical equipment
  • Follow evacuation orders from authorities
  • Keep listening to emergency broadcasts

After a Flood

  • Wait for authorities to declare it safe to return
  • Avoid floodwater as it may be contaminated
  • Document damage with photos for insurance
  • Clean and disinfect everything that got wet
  • Watch for structural damage before entering buildings
